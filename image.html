Lamborghini Veneno Specifications

Overview

The Lamborghini Veneno is a high-performance, limited-edition supercar created to celebrate Lamborghini's 50th anniversary.

Specifications

Engine 6.5-liter V12
Power 750 horsepower (552 kW)
Torque 690 Nm (509 lb-ft)
Transmission 7-speed automated manual (ISR)
0-60 mph (0-100 km/h) Approximately 2.8 seconds
Top Speed Around 221 mph (356 km/h)
Weight About 1,490 kg (3,278 lbs)
Body Carbon fiber monocoque chassis
Price Originally around $4.5 million USD
